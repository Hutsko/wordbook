* Another trendy term in this field is apoptosis, which is derived from Latin for something like “falling off” (as in the falling off of leaves in the fall, an example of programmed death). There are great debates as to whether apoptosis equals programmed cell death or is just a subtype of it (I subscribe to the latter view), as well as, amazingly, whether you pronounce the second p in the word (I do, a pronunciation that is considered to have a rough-hewn man-in-the-street plebian air to it).
